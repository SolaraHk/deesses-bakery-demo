The Story

A bakery that chases the light.

This is a design concept — a love letter to golden crumb and honeyed crust. The look borrows from quiet morning markets and the soft glow that lands on a loaf just as the city wakes up.

Long, patient ferments

Dough left to find its own flavour overnight, until the crust caramelises into deep amber.

Pastry, laminated by hand

Butter and flour folded into countless layers that shatter, lift, and shine.

Rooted in Hong Kong

An imagined neighbourhood bakery with one foot in the wetlands and one in the harbour.
